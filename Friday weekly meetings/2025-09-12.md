Lauren Keenan - MAUI Screen Time App
- digital wellbeing app that tracks screen time on apps like social media and uses screen time minutes to calculate co2eq and compares costs to events in the world
- feature and benefits: safe and performant background app operating silently, use Widley accepted co2 conversions, create relative comparisons to natural world events. this should empower users to better understand their online activity, help interrupt cycles of social media overuse and addiction, inspire curiosity to the natural world
- scope and deliverables: permissions, tracking and storage, calculations, display
- research and design - legal aspects, research, Design (HCI), Development
- using .NET MAUI
